The Industry Problem

Clients are tightening requirements — and firms running on outdated systems can't keep up.

The pressure is clear: reduce cycle times, lower loss adjustment expenses, and back it up with real-time reporting and analytics. Firms still operating on spreadsheets, shared drives, and disconnected tools aren't losing because of their adjusters — they're losing because their systems weren't built for what clients are demanding today.

Problem 01
Piecemeal Technology
Intake in one system. Assignments over email. File tracking in spreadsheets. Reporting pulled manually. Firms have bolted together tools that were never designed to work together — and every seam is a gap where cycle time expands and LAE climbs.
Problem 02
Operational Misguidance
Firms spend the majority of their operational overhead behind closed doors — managing files, chasing status updates, and putting out internal fires. INSPEKTiQ frees your operations and sales teams to focus where it matters: growing client relationships and expanding your book of business.
Problem 03
No Proof of Performance
Your client needs to justify your LAE. They need cycle time data, file status visibility, and submission tracking — and your current systems can't produce any of it. Without proof of efficiency, you're just another line item to cut.
